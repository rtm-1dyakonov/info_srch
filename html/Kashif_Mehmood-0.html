Kashif Mehmood (Urdu: کاشف محمود) is a Pakistani television actor known for his villainous roles in television serials, including Ashiyana, Landa Bazar, Moorat, Bilqees Kaur, Naagin, and Is Chand Pay Dagh Nahin. He has also acted in some Pakistani films.[1][2][3]
