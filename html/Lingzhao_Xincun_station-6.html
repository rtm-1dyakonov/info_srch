Lingzhao Xincun (Chinese: 凌兆新村; pinyin: Língzhào Xīncūn) is a station on Line 8 of the Shanghai Metro. This station is part of the southern extension of Line 8 and opened on July 5, 2009.

It is located near the junction of Jiyang Road and Lingzhao Road, in Shanghai's Pudong New Area.
